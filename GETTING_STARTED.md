# Getting Started

Welcome to your AI Coaching App! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Quick Start

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account to start exploring coaches
2. **Discover**: Browse pre-built coaches on the Discover tab
3. **Chat**: Tap any coach to start a coaching session
4. **Create**: Build your own custom AI coach
5. **Profile**: Add your personal context for better guidance

## First Time Setup

### 1. Create Your Account

On the signup screen, enter:
- Your name (how coaches will address you)
- Email address
- Password (minimum 6 characters)

### 2. Complete Your Profile

After signing up, go to the Profile tab and add:

**Personal Context** (recommended)
- Your background and current situation
- What you're working on
- Challenges you're facing
- Goals you want to achieve

**Core Values**
- What matters most to you
- Principles that guide your decisions
- Values you want coaches to respect

Example:
```
Personal Context: "I'm a founder building a productivity app.
I struggle with managing my time between product development,
sales, and team building. Looking to create better systems
and improve my leadership skills."

Core Values: Growth mindset, Work-life balance, Authenticity,
Innovation, Team success
```

This context helps coaches provide more relevant, personalized guidance.

### 3. Explore Sample Coaches

The app comes with three pre-built coaches:

**Alex Rivera** - Productivity & Systems Coach
- Perfect for: Building workflows, time management, automation
- Style: Practical, analytical, results-oriented

**Maya Chen** - Leadership & Strategy Coach
- Perfect for: Leadership development, strategic thinking, team building
- Style: Insightful, empathetic, challenging

**Jordan Taylor** - Wellness & Mindfulness Coach
- Perfect for: Stress management, work-life balance, mindfulness
- Style: Calming, supportive, holistic

Tap any coach to see their full profile and start chatting.

## Using Coaches

### Starting a Conversation

1. Browse coaches on the Discover tab
2. Tap a coach to view their profile
3. Tap "Start Coaching Session"
4. Begin chatting immediately

### Chat Tips

**Be Specific**: The more context you provide, the better guidance you'll receive.

Bad: "I need help with productivity"
Good: "I'm spending 3 hours a day in meetings and struggling to find time for deep work. How can I better manage my calendar?"

**Ask Follow-up Questions**: Coaches are here to help you explore and think deeper.

**Share Challenges**: Be honest about what's not working. Coaches are non-judgmental.

**Request Examples**: Ask for specific examples or actionable steps when needed.

## Creating Your Own Coach

### Why Create a Custom Coach?

- Design a coach for your specific needs
- Create a coach with expertise in your field
- Build a coach with a personality that resonates with you
- Share coaches with others

### Creation Steps

1. Go to the Create tab
2. Fill in the coach details:

**Name**: Give your coach a name (e.g., "Sarah Chen")

**Title**: Their role/expertise (e.g., "Product Design Coach")

**Description**: What they help with and their approach

**Specialties**: Add 3-5 areas of expertise
- Be specific (e.g., "User Research" not just "Design")
- Think about what you want help with

**Personality Traits**: How they coach
- Examples: Patient, Direct, Encouraging, Analytical

**System Prompt**: Instructions for how the AI should behave
- Write as "You are..."
- Include their expertise and approach
- Mention how they should interact

Example System Prompt:
```
You are Sarah Chen, a product design coach with 15 years
of experience. You help designers and product managers
create user-centered products. Your approach is empathetic
and collaborative. Always start by understanding the user's
problem deeply before suggesting solutions. Ask probing
questions about user needs, business goals, and constraints.
Provide specific frameworks and examples when helpful.
```

3. Choose visibility:
   - Public: Others can discover and use your coach
   - Private: Only you can access this coach

4. Tap "Create Coach"

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Coaches provide better guidance when they understand your context

2. **Be Consistent**: Regular check-ins with coaches help build momentum

3. **Try Different Coaches**: Each has a unique approach. Find what works for you.

4. **Iterate on Custom Coaches**: If a coach you created isn't quite right, update the system prompt

5. **Share Conversations**: Continue conversations across multiple sessions. Context is maintained.

## Common Questions

**Can coaches see my other conversations?**
No, each conversation is independent.

**How do I delete a conversation?**
Currently, conversations are permanent. This will be added in a future update.

**Can I edit a coach after creating it?**
Not yet, but this feature is coming soon.

**What happens to my data?**
Your data is securely stored in Supabase with row-level security. Only you can access your conversations and profile.
